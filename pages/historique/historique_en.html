history of SFR

1.Brief chronology of SFR from 1987 to 2018

The french society of Radiotéléphone, SFR, is a French telecommunications operator branch bringing together the telecoms activities of Altice de France. This company was created by the General Water Company in 1987.

Owned entirely by Vivendi since 2011, it was bought by ALTICE in 2014, to form the Numericable_SFR group. In 2016 SFR GROUPa Telecom, Media and Advertising group is born. The Numericable brand is only used by the group for its bouquet of television channels.

In May 2017, the Altice group announced its intention to consolidate most of its activities under its name. As part of this merger, SFR will be renamed Altice6 Thus, on , the SFR mobile network takes the name SFR Altice in order to accustom customers to the name planned to be adopted by SFR: Altice7.
The idea was finally abandoned in March 2018. following the setbacks (decline in share price and financial results) of SFR in 2017. Altice wishes to maintain a brand with a very strong reputation with the aim of improving its results for the months and years to come.

2. The key players involved in the creation of the SFR and the important stages of its history


2.1 The key players involved

Alain bravo is a French polytechnic engineer and entrepreneur born April 1, 1945.
And was an important actor in the creation of the SFR. He led the group that developed the initial project
and played a key role in the negotiations with the French authorities to obtain the operator license.

Didier Quillot born in Mas-Grenier, is a French businessman. And was also a key figure in the formation of the SFR.
He held management positions within SFR and helped guide the company's commercial and operational strategy during its early years.

Jean-Paul Cottet was an important actor in the process of creating the SFR. He brought his expertise in the field of telecommunications
and contributed to the establishment of the infrastructures and technologies necessary for the operation of the company.

2.2 the important stages of its history

*Beginning of mobile services (1987)

In December 1987, SFR launched its first mobile services and began marketing mobile telephone subscriptions in France.

* The IPO (2000)

In November 2000, SFR-Cegetel was listed on the stock exchange.
At this time, Vodafone becomes one of the main shareholders.

* Acquisition by Vivendi (2002)

Vivendi, led by Jean-Marie Messier, acquired a majority stake in SFR-Cegetel in 2002.

* Reorganization and name change (2005)

In 2005, the company was renamed SFR, abandoning the acronym Cegetel.
